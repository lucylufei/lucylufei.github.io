Magnetic Tracking Rover

A rover is built from a blank breadboard, wheels, and sheet metal to track a preprogrammed wire generating a magnetic field detected by inductors. The wire can be user-controlled for real time signals, tracked by the rover to complete commands such as 180 turns.

SolidWorks Exploration

Teams generate ideas for devices aiding the life of a person with limited motor abilities. Prototypes were created using common stationery, drafted on SolidWorks, and finally 3-D printed.



Simple RISC Machine

A turing complete RISC machine created through Verilog code and synthesized in Quartus, capable of reading binary instructions. Implemented on a FPGA, successfully executing Fibonacci sequence code written in C with top 10 execution time of 100+ groups.

Cardboard Chair

Schools in rural Africa struggle with seats for students due to the mobility in location. Teams of 6 design chairs made using only cardboard as a solution for cheap and simple seats. Chairs are weight tested then scored by design and comfort.

Rain Harvesting Analysis

A small town on Texada Island, BC wishes to rely on rain water throughout the year. Excel calculations are used to determine the best choices in piping, tank, pipes, etc, to harvest water to maintain the water consumption lifestyle of Texada citizens without BC Hydro.

Tic Tac Toe Game

My first project using Verilog involved the creation of a tic-tac-toe game in which the computer never loses. The code was tested on ModelSim and synthesized on Quartus. It was then implemented on a SoC and displayed through VGA to be user interactive.

Alarm Clock

The alarm clock was created on a breadboard using a microprocessor coded with 8051 assembly. Users can set the clock displayed through LED and program alarm times through a simple finite state machine. The speaker sounds each hour and at alarm times with an option for snooze.

ATMEL Microprocessor Projects

Various projects were completed using 8051 assembly on a breadboard with an ATMEL microprocessor. This includes a thermometer, a RMS/peak voltage calculator, a motor controller, and more.
